About

Hi, my name is Philip Trauner.

I'm a cynical computer science student residing in Austria. I do stuff on the interwebs™.

I can get comfortable with the basics of programming languages pretty quickly and primarily enjoy working in high-level ones like Python, Swift, JavaScript and C# (and despise Java). Recently I've also gotten to grips with Rust (which is pretty awesome). I'm still missing a big C or C++ project in my repertoire, but that will come up for sure at some point.

I'm proficient in all three major operating systems (Linux, macOS, Windows) and would rather be homeless than use Windows on a productivity-machine.

If you want to talk to me, visit me in my doctor's office (has nothing to do with medicine, just an escalated inside joke) or shoot me an e-mail to my contact address.

I guess that's it for now. If I get around to it I might update some of this later down the road (I won't).
